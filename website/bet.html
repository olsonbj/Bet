    • About RattTrapp

    RatTrapp is a community based hip-hop group who made their debut on the scene by releasing their hit single Head & Shoulders. The group is based out of Rosemount, MN, and is focused on embodying the spirit of hip-hop by including all different people on their songs. Their highly anticipated mixtape "camelCase" is expected to be released by the end of 2018.
